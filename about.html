About Woelab

Our mission is to empower communities through open source collaboration and learning.

Who we are

Woelab is a community-driven workshop where people learn, build, and contribute to open source projects. We provide hands-on sessions, mentorship, and resources to help contributors at all levels.

What we do

We host events, maintain educational materials, and support collaborative projects that solve real-world problems.
